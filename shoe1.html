"Animal Pack - Cheetah"

R9 299


The Nike Dunk Low PRM “Cheetah” is one of two safari-themed releases of the low-top shoe by Nike in Spring 2021. From Nike’s “Animal Pack” that also includes a “Zebra” colorway, the “Cheetah” brings a premium look to the beloved former basketball and current lifestyle shoe by employing a color block reminiscent of the colors found on the fast feline. Nike dresses the Dunk’s base in cream leather and applies subtle spots to the light brown suede overlays found on the forefoot, eyelets, collar, and heel. A slightly glossy dark brown leather Swoosh appears on both sides and “Nike” detailing is embroidered onto the heel. Brown “Nike” branding is located on white tongue tag found on the top of the cream-colored nylon tongue. Cream laces and a brown inner lining continue the neutral based look of the shoe. Nike supplies the “Cheetah” Dunk with a white rubber midsole and brown outsole. Release date: June 22, 2021
