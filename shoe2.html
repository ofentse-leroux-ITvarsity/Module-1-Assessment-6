YEEZY BOOST 350 V2

"Mono Ice"

R8 999


The adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 “Mono Ice” is a June 2021 release by Kanye West and adidas that reimagines the popular lifestyle shoe with a one-piece translucent mesh construction. A completely new look for the Yeezy Boost 350 V2, the “Mono Ice” does away with the 350 V2’s original woven Primeknit design in favor of a new translucent mesh upper that obscures a web cage underneath. Despite the new design, the “Mono Ice” retains the monofilament stripe found on several late-model 350 V2s. The white inner lining and laces contrast the icy look of the shoe. Underfoot, a white translucent ribbed midsole houses adidas’s Boost cushioning technology. Release date: June 21, 2021.
