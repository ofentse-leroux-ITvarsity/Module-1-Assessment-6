The Nike Air Fear of God 1 “Triple Black” is an essential for fans of Jerry Lorenzo’s upscale fashion label, Fear of God, and for those who enjoy the moody all-black-everything aesthetic. Lorenzo joined a very short list of non-athletes who have created their own silhouettes under the Nike umbrella when he debuted the Air Fear of God 1 in 2017. The hybrid performance hoops and lifestyle shoe sports a black suede overlay on the toe while tonal nylon graces the collar, heel, and mid-panel. The staggered lacing system is augmented by a synthetic tonal overlay on the heel. A smoky midsole with tonal Fear of God and Nike Air branding puts the finishing touches on the stealthy look. Release date: April 25, 2020.
